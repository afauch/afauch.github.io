Pride & Joy

Pride & Joy is a solo electronic music project.

Influenced by Chicago’s footwork and bop genres, Pride & Joy deals with questions of contemporary spirituality, modern love, masculinity, and technology.

Pride & Joy has released four solo LPs and EPs to date.

  • You Leave Me with No Words to Say 2014
  • Starfall (for Jackie) 2014
  • Sunday / Anything for You EP 2013
  • Pride & Joy EP 2013

Much of my multimedia work as Pride & Joy involves commentary on the relationship between soul, body, and technological progress.
