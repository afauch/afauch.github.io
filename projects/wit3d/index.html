Wit3D

Conversation is one of the most natural means of interaction, and voice-based interfaces will play a significant role in future UI design. I built the Wit3D C# utility to give designers the power of natural language processing in their AR/VR applications.

Fig 2

Wit3D allows Unity, the industry-standard AR/VR interaction suite, to interface with Wit.ai - Facebook's natural language application platform.

Fig 1

Wit.ai is a powerful NLP engine that allows developers to create voice-controlled applications. Voice commands originate in Unity, Wit.ai parses the audio to understand intent, and returns this intent to Unity in JSON format.

Fig 2

Because it integrates with Unity, Wit3D brings voice control to augmented reality and virtual reality applications. This example demonstrates the use of Wit3D in a Vuforia application.

As mixed-reality objects become more common, users must have the ability to interact with these digital objects intuitively.

Wit3D is an early attempt to bring the power of conversational UI into the world of AR/VR design.
