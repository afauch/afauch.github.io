Shapes

In 2017, I was approached by social VR firm High Fidelity to redesign their 3D creation tools. Over the course of 3 months, I led the design process from exploratory user research, through iterative prototyping, usability testing, and beta shipment in summer 2017.

Within a few days of beta release, Shapes quickly rose to the top of the 'most downloaded' apps on High Fidelity's app store.
