• Philosophy ––

Fundamentally, mixed reality is about our relationship to self.

The purpose of a tool is to bring an intention – a value – into the world. Recently, tools have begun to shape human intentions, rather than the other way around. As a designer, I believe mixed reality is an opportunity to reclaim our tools; to reexamine ourselves, and the values we hope to embody.
